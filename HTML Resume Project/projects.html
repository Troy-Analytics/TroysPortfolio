Favorite Sports Survey
  • Overview: The sports survey form is a web-based questionnaire designed to collect information about participants' favorite sports, preferences for watching sports, and reasons for their choices. It allows users to provide their name, email, age, gender, recommendations for sports, preferred methods of watching sports, favorite sports, and additional comments.

  • Technologies Used: HTML, CSS

  • Key Features:
    • The form is well-structured with labels and input fields for capturing relevant information.
    • Certain fields like name, email, and recommendations are marked as required to ensure data completeness.
    • The form includes dropdown menus and checkboxes for selecting gender, preferred methods of watching sports, and favorite sports.
    • A textarea field is provided for participants to share their reasons for loving sports.
    • A submit button is included to allow users to submit their responses.

  • Outcome: The sports survey form provides an interactive and user-friendly way for participants to share their preferences and opinions related to sports. It facilitates data collection for analyzing trends, preferences, and feedback from participants. The outcome of the survey could be used for various purposes such as market research, improving sports programming, or tailoring sports-related services to better meet users' needs.
