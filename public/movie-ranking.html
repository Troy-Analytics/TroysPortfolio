All time movie reviews by Troy

My top 3 favorite movies

1. Interstellar(2014)

Director: Christopher Nolan
Genre: Sci-Fi/Drama
Runtime: 2hr 49m
Rating: PG-13

"Interstellar" isn't just a movie; it's a cosmic symphony of sights and emotions that will leave you breathless. It's a cinematic masterpiece that doesn't just entertain; it challenges your mind, tugs at your heartstrings, and ignites a sense of wonder that lingers long after the credits roll. With its breathtaking visuals, thought-provoking themes, and epic scope spanning the cosmos, "Interstellar" isn't just a film—it's an experience. It demands to be seen, felt, and savored, as it transports you to the furthest reaches of space and the deepest corners of your soul. So buckle up, because this journey isn't just about where we go—it's about who we are, and where we dare to dream.

2. Ready Player One(2018)

Director: Steven Spielberg
Genre: Sci-Fi/Special Interest
Runtime: 2hr 20m
Rating: PG-13

Alright, fellow geeks, buckle up for a wild ride through the OASIS in "Ready Player One" This movie is like a geek's dream come true, with more pop culture references than you can shake a lightsaber at. From nostalgic nods to classic video games and '80s movies to epic quests that'll have you shouting 'Excelsior!', it's an adventure-packed thrill ride from start to finish. And let's talk about those visuals—Steven Spielberg takes us on a CGI joyride through a virtual wonderland that'll have you drooling over your retro game controller. But hey, it's not just about the pixels and polygons; at its core, "Ready Player One" is a heartfelt story about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination. So grab your popcorn, dust off your old Atari, and get ready to geek out like never before.

3. The Lost World: Jurassic Park(1997)

Director: Steven Spielberg
Genre: Sci-Fi
Runtime: 2hr 09m
Rating: PG-13

Welcome back to Isla Sorna, folks, where the dinosaurs are bigger, badder, and ready to wreak havoc in "The Lost World: Jurassic Park" Hold onto your butts because this sequel is like a T-Rex on a caffeine binge—non-stop action, bigger stakes, and more teeth than your dentist's worst nightmare. Sure, the humans might be running around like scared little raptors, but who cares when you've got Jeff Goldblum's signature chaos theory swagger stealing the show? And let's not forget about those dino-mite special effects; the CGI dinos are so realistic, you'll swear you can hear them roar. Plus, with Steven Spielberg back in the director's chair, you know you're in for a wild ride through the jungle. So grab your night vision goggles, watch out for sneaky velociraptors, and get ready for a dino-tastic adventure that'll leave you roaring for more.
